J.A.N.E. FAQs

How do I install J.A.N.E.?

Open the Chrome Web Store and click Add to Chrome, then pin the icon for quick access.

Which sites are supported?

LinkedIn and Indeed today. Many public job pages also work.

What data do you see?

Only the job page where you open the panel. We do not store your resume or cover-letter content on our servers.

The panel did not appear. What should I try?

Refresh, click the toolbar icon, ensure the extension is enabled, and reopen the tab if needed.

How do I share feedback?

Use the feedback form below or message us on LinkedIn. Screenshots help.

FAQ

Is the internship paid?

Many are unpaid or stipend based. We focus on training, portfolio credit, and strong references. If you need a specific arrangement, tell us in the form.

Location

Remote first with US overlap preferred. Occasional in-person days may happen when the team is in the same city.

Duration

6 to 12 weeks is common. We also do short sprints if you want to try a focused project.

International students

Work must follow your visa rules. If you are on OPT or CPT, include that detail in the form.
